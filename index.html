Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find a selection of the most frequently asked questions. You can always contact us using the form below if you need more information

  • Is it anonymous feedback?

    This is a setting decided by your company. The feedback can be anonymous, with the name specified or you can choose by yourself.

  • The platform allows relative feedback?

    Yes, It is possible to make the feedback compared among the team or the company as a whole

  • In which forms the feedback is shared?

    The feedback provider can use not only the traditional text message but also, a voice message, a video or include emojis and pictures

  • Can the manager have access to the team feedback?

    Yes, in the settings the company can decide who will be allowed to consult the feedback and when

  • Is there a dashboard for every person?

    Yes, every person has her own dashboard where they can see the whole feedback flow and graphs with the main trends
